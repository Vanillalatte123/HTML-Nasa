Social Media



Instagram

An Instagram bio is the small area underneath your username for you to share some details about yourself or your brand. Your Instagram bio can include a brief self or brand description, contact information, emojis, hashtags and more. Here's an example of the best bio on Instagram: Marie Forleo has a strong Insta bio.

Facebook

Facebook is a website which allows users, who sign-up for free profiles, to connect with friends, work colleagues or people they don't know, online. It allows users to share pictures, music, videos, and articles, as well as their own thoughts and opinions with however many people they like.
